Tadoba Resort club is one of the most ideal holiday Resort, with simple and reasonably priced retreat that is just a few minutes away (200 mtrs) from the Moharli gate or the main entrance of the Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve. Clean and comfortable rooms, impeccable service and homely atmosphere make our Resort a perfect option for the guests. We take pride in the fact that we use best practices to ensure we do not leave any carbon foot print their by ensuring the least amount of damage to the surroundings.

Located in a culturally rich and naturally pristine environment the Resort is not just a hotel but a home away from home. The entrance of the Resort is lined with trees on both sides, leading to 13 luxury air conditioned rooms with modern amenities. Where in our each deluxe rooms has Veranda over-looking the green lawns.

At Tadoba Resort club we have 8 deluxe rooms named Bamboo, Woods, Sambar, Tiger, Peacock, Indian Roller, Monkey and Bison, 4 standard rooms on ground floor and first floor and 1 Family unit for the group. Each room has been designed keeping in mind that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. Offering the very best of hospitality, heart-warming service and other delightful facilities, this holiday retreat boasts of its elegant outlook and dedication towards the essence of a peaceful holiday. The attentive service and genuine hospitality is one of the highlights of our Resort.

The evenings can be enjoyed out in the open Lawn with evening tea and snacks after coming back with the encounter of the wilderness of Park, or you can just relax there on the green lawn overlooking the awesome sunset which can be seen from our Resort specially. At night the bundle of tiny white lights ( stars ) on your head will definitely make you fall in love with Mother Nature and guarantees to make your stay unforgettable.

A lot of the work at Tadoba Resort club is done by the rural people of Tadoba and our most of the staff is from the same village, which is our contribution to rural public participation for development of area as well as responsible tourism.

Now it comes to the most delicious part of our property i.e. food. Yes, we have our own unique great flavours which we are maintaining from the beginning. Our approach is simple. We have a passion for pure flavour, quality ingredients and for creating an atmosphere that is jovial, and a haven for people who simply love food. Hygiene and nutritional value is very well maintained. You will feel homely touch to our every dish.

We provide both veg food (herbivore) and non–veg food (carnivore) which is according to our AP plan. We have buffet/ fixed menu. If you wish to have something of your choice, our chef would definitely prepare it, but on prior notice as we have to bring most of the things from the market which is 25 km away. Each of our dish is created to pamper you almost. Because we know that, “One cannot think well, love well and sleep well if one has not dined well." So, let’s rediscover and rejuvenate yourself in the wilderness to Tadoba with Royal Tiger Resort.

“Our goal is that, you leave feeling better, than when you arrived!”
